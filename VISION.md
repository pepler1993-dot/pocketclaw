# PocketClaw Vision

## What PocketClaw is

PocketClaw is a mobile app that makes OpenClaw usable directly on smartphones through a simple, modern, and highly user-friendly interface.

The goal is not to expose raw OpenClaw complexity to users. The goal is to hide the complexity behind a product that feels approachable, fast, understandable, and useful.

PocketClaw should allow people to set up, run, and use OpenClaw locally on their device without needing to understand terminals, config files, networking, or server administration.

## Core idea

**OpenClaw for normal people.**

That means:
- local-first where possible
- mobile-first UX
- privacy-aware by default
- setup without technical friction
- voice interaction as a first-class feature

## Who it is for

- users who want a local AI assistant on their phone
- users who value privacy and local control
- users who are interested in automation but do not want to manage server infrastructure
- power users who still want advanced settings when needed

## Product promise

PocketClaw should make OpenClaw feel less like infrastructure and more like a real product.

The app should let people:
- install and launch their assistant
- connect models and services
- configure tools and channels
- talk to the assistant by voice or text
- manage everything from a clean mobile UI

## Long-term direction

PocketClaw should evolve into the simplest way to run and manage OpenClaw on personal devices.

Not a sysadmin tool.
Not a developer toy.
A real mobile product.
